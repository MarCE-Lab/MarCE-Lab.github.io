Welcome to professor Rih-Teng Wu's Laboratory at National Taiwan University. Our group conducts interdisciplinary research to develop data analytics tools and artificial intelligence-based solutions for condition assessment and design of structures, which are key elements toward achieving sustainable and resilient civil infrastructure systems. Using our proposed methodologies, we are one step closer to implementing the vision of smart cities.
